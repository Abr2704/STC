Welcome to STC

Specialist Technical College is a British technical institution operating in the United Kingdom and Kosovo. STC offers university-level computing qualifications.

Our programmes provide the skills needed for the IT industry but with much needed globally recognised routes that allow students to progress directly into British and Irish universities.

British-Standard Programmes at Level 3, Level 4 and Level 5

Our curriculum follows recognised UK vocational frameworks across Levels 3, 4 and 5, providing structured progression from foundational knowledge to advanced technical capability.

  • Level 3 (A-Level Equivalent): Builds core computing understanding and essential study skills.

  • Level 4 (First Year of University): Strengthens technical competence and professional practice.

  • Level 5 (Second Year of University): Develops advanced specialist skills and prepares learners for entry into final-year BSc programmes.

Teaching and Assessment

Teaching is delivered through a blend of classroom sessions, practical workshops and guided independent study. Assessment typically includes assignments, projects and practical tasks, reflecting British vocational approaches and encouraging learners to apply their skills in realistic scenarios.

Partnerships and Collaboration

University Partnerships

We are actively developing partnerships with British universities to bring selected degree programmes directly on-site in Kosovo. These collaborations will allow students to complete internationally recognised UK degrees locally, making high-quality university education significantly more accessible and affordable.

Exam Board Collaboration

We work closely with recognised UK awarding bodies to adapt and refine qualifications in line with employer demand and industry trends. This collaborative approach ensures our computing curriculum remains relevant, up-to-date and aligned with the evolving needs of the global technology sector.
