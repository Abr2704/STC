Welcome to Specialist Technical College

Specialist Technical College is a British technical institution operating in the United Kingdom and Kosovo. We offer university-level computing qualifications that blend applied learning with recognised pathways so students can progress directly into British and Irish universities.

Our focus is simple: provide accessible, high-quality technical education that prepares confident, work-ready graduates for an international labour market.

Why Study with Us?

  • Globally recognised qualifications with routes into British and Irish universities.
  • Flexible modular study, including weekend options for professionals and expats.
  • Smaller class sizes with personalised, supportive teaching.

Levels and Progression

  • Level 3 (A-Level Equivalent): Builds core computing understanding and essential study skills.

  • Level 4 (First Year of University): Strengthens technical competence and professional practice.

  • Level 5 (Second Year of University): Develops advanced specialist skills and prepares learners for entry into final-year BSc programmes.

Teaching and Assessment

Teaching blends classroom sessions, practical workshops, and guided independent study. Assessment includes assignments, projects, and practical tasks, reflecting British vocational approaches and encouraging learners to apply their skills in realistic scenarios.
