Welcome to Specialist Technical College

Specialist Technical College is a British technical college based in Kosovo, offering high-quality technical and vocational education aligned with UK standards.

Our programmes are designed to develop the knowledge, skills and professional behaviours required by modern industries. We combine a British approach to teaching and assessment with a strong understanding of the local context, so that graduates are well-prepared for employment, further study or international progression routes.

Academic Focus

British-Standard Programmes

Our curriculum is informed by British vocational qualifications and guided by best practice from UK colleges. Learners are supported to develop technical competence, problem-solving skills and confidence in real-world applications.

Key Areas of Study

  • Computing & IT – networking, software, digital skills.
  • Health & Social Care – foundation knowledge for care sectors.
  • Electronics – circuits, components, systems and fault-finding.

Teaching and Assessment

Teaching is delivered through a blend of classroom sessions, practical workshops and guided independent study. Assessment typically includes assignments, projects and practical tasks, reflecting British vocational approaches and encouraging learners to apply their skills in realistic scenarios.
