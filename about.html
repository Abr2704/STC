About Specialist Technical College

Specialist Technical College is a British technical college based in Kosovo, providing vocational and technical education inspired by UK practice. We focus on developing competent, confident and work-ready learners who are equipped to contribute to the local economy and to succeed in an increasingly international labour market.

Our British Identity

The college draws on British approaches to curriculum, teaching and assessment. Our programmes are designed and delivered in line with British vocational standards, placing emphasis on applied learning, independent study, and the development of transferable skills such as communication, teamwork and problem solving.

By aligning with British educational principles, we aim to offer learners in Kosovo a high-quality, internationally recognisable style of technical education, while remaining grounded in the needs of the local context.

Mission and Aims

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality technical and vocational education that supports individual progression and regional development.

We aim to:

  • Deliver programmes that reflect British standards of quality and rigour.
  • Prepare learners for employment, further study or international pathways.
  • Work in partnership with employers and education providers.
  • Promote professional attitudes, ethical practice and lifelong learning.

Programmes We Offer

The college offers a growing portfolio of technical programmes, initially focused on:

  • Computing & IT – digital skills, networking, software and systems.
  • Cyber Security – specialist pathways in protecting systems and data.
  • Electronics – circuits, components, diagnostics and practical applications.

Programmes are developed with reference to UK vocational frameworks and are regularly reviewed to ensure they remain relevant and up to date.

Why Study with Us in Kosovo?

  • Access a British-style technical education without leaving Kosovo.
  • Study in small groups with structured academic and pastoral support.
  • Develop practical skills through workshops, projects and lab activity.
  • Gain a strong foundation for employment or further study, locally or abroad.
