Computing & IT

The Computing & IT pathway at Specialist Technical College provides a structured route from Level 3 through to Level 5, aligned with British vocational standards. Learners develop practical digital skills, underpinning knowledge and professional behaviours needed for roles in IT support, networking, software and related fields.

Programmes combine classroom teaching, hands-on lab work and guided independent study, with an emphasis on applied learning and real-world scenarios.

Level 3 Computing & IT

Level 3 provides a foundation in core computing concepts and practical digital skills. It is suitable for learners progressing from school or for those seeking a structured introduction to the sector.

Indicative Modules

  • Fundamentals of Computer Systems
  • Introduction to Networking and Internet Technologies
  • Using Office and Productivity Software
  • Introduction to Programming (e.g. Python)
  • Website Fundamentals
  • Professional Practice and Digital Citizenship

Tuition fee: €600

Fees shown are per academic year.

Level 4 Computing & IT

Level 4 builds on the foundation developed at Level 3 and introduces more advanced technical content. It is designed for learners who wish to progress towards higher-level technical roles or further study.

Indicative Modules

  • Advanced Networking Concepts
  • Database Design and Implementation
  • Systems Analysis and Design
  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Operating Systems and Virtualisation
  • Professional and Academic Skills for IT

Tuition fee: €1,400

Fees shown are per academic year.

Level 5 Computing & IT

Level 5 deepens technical expertise and focuses on applying knowledge in more complex and independent projects. It supports progression into higher-level employment or top-up study at degree level or equivalent.

Indicative Modules

  • Network Security and Cybersecurity Fundamentals
  • Advanced Database and Data Management
  • Cloud Services and Infrastructure Concepts
  • Software Development Project
  • IT Service Management and Support
  • Emerging Technologies in Computing

Tuition fee: €1,400

Fees shown are per academic year.

Entry and Progression

Entry requirements and progression routes are explained in more detail in our Admissions information. In broad terms:

  • Level 3 is appropriate for learners progressing from school or equivalent.
  • Level 4 normally requires successful completion of a suitable Level 3 qualification or equivalent experience.
  • Level 5 normally requires successful completion of Level 4 in a relevant area.

For personalised guidance, prospective learners are encouraged to contact the college for an information, advice and guidance discussion.
