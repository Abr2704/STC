Level 3 – A-Level Equivalent

Level 3 programmes provide an A-Level equivalent route into British-style technical education in Kosovo.


They are designed for learners who want to build strong foundations in Computing (Cyber Security) before progressing to higher-level study.


Successful completion of Level 3 can lead directly into our Level 4 & 5 university-level programmes in Computing and Cyber Security, and then on to a final year BSc (Top-Up) with partner universities.

Course Tuition Fee: €600 per Level 3 programme, €900 for two Level 3 programmes studied concurrently.

ATHE Level 3 – Introduction to Cyber Security

This Level 3 qualification introduces the core ideas of cyber security: how online systems can be attacked, how information can be protected, and how to keep yourself and others safe when working online.


Key Topics

  • Cyber threats, attacks & vulnerabilities.
  • Safe online behaviour & authentication.
  • Basic security on devices & networks.
  • Risk management in daily digital use.


Programme Snapshot

  • Level: 3 (A-Level equivalent)
  • Assessment: Assignments & practical tasks.

Entry Requirements for Level 3

  • Normally aged 16+ or an adult learner.
  • Completion of compulsory secondary education.
  • Interest in Computing, IT or Cyber Security.
  • Basic computer skills.
  • English GCSE and Maths GCSE or equivalent (support available).
