Level 3 (A-Level Equivalent) Programmes

Our Level 3 programmes provide an A-Level equivalent route into higher study in Computing, Cyber Security and related technical fields. They are ideal for learners who want a British-style technical pathway in Kosovo and plan to progress to Level 4 and 5 university-level study.

Typical tuition fee: €600 per Level 3 programme.

ATHE Level 3 – Introduction to Cyber Security

Awarding Body: ATHE Level 3 Cyber Security

This short Level 3 qualification introduces learners to the core ideas of cyber security: how online systems can be attacked, how information can be protected, and how to keep yourself and others safe when working online. It provides a strong foundation for further study in cyber security and computing. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Key Topics

  • What cyber security is and why it matters.
  • Common cyber threats, attacks and vulnerabilities.
  • Safe online behaviour, passwords and authentication.
  • Basic security measures on phones, laptops and networks.
  • Managing cyber security risks in everyday life and work.

Programme Information

  • Level: 3 (A-Level equivalent)
  • Mode: Classroom-based with guided independent study.
  • Assessment: Assignments and practical activities.
  • Typical Tuition Fee: €600

BCS Level 3 IT (Flexiqual Route)

Awarding Body: BCS Level 3 Computing & IT

This Level 3 IT programme is designed to build strong digital and technical skills across a range of areas such as productivity tools, networks, databases and web technologies. The content can be adapted to meet local needs and employer demand, providing a flexible route into further study or employment in IT.

Example Units (Placeholders)

The exact units will be confirmed with the British Computer Society. For now, we are using generic unit titles as placeholders; these can be updated later with the official unit names.

  • Unit 1: Productivity Software and Office IT
  • Unit 2: IT Systems and Technical Support
  • Unit 3: Fundamentals of Computer Networking
  • Unit 4: Databases and Data Handling
  • Unit 5: Web and Digital Content Creation

Programme Information

  • Level: 3 (A-Level equivalent)
  • Mode: Blended learning – classroom sessions plus practical labs.
  • Assessment: Assignments, practical tasks and portfolio work.
  • Typical Tuition Fee: €600

Progression from Level 3

  • Progress to Level 4 Computing / Cyber Security.
  • Develop core IT and problem-solving skills.

Admissions
