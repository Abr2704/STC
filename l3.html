Level 3 Certificate and Diploma – A-Level Equivalents

Successful completion of Level 3 can lead directly into our Level 4 & 5 university-level programmes in Computing and Cyber Security, and then on to a final year BSc (Top-Up) with partner universities. They are designed for learners who want to build strong foundations in Computing (Cyber Security) before progressing to higher-level study.

ATHE Level 3 Certificate (Intro to Cyber Security)

This Level 3 qualification introduces the core ideas of cyber security: how online systems can be attacked, how information can be protected, and how to keep yourself and others safe when working online.
